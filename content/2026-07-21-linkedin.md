We spent this month auditing 5 consulting firms' websites. All of them had the same 3 problems:

1. Zero AI integration. No chatbot, no smart forms, no automated follow-up. Every lead required a human to respond within business hours.

2. Generic messaging. Their value props could belong to any agency. Nothing made them stand out.

3. No conversion measurement. They had Google Analytics installed but weren't tracking what mattered: form submissions, phone calls, chat interactions.

The fix for each took less than a week. The results: 2-3x more qualified leads within 30 days.

The gap between a "good enough" website and a revenue-generating platform has never been smaller — or more profitable to close.
