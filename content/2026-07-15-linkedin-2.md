Unpopular opinion: your traffic problem is usually a conversion problem wearing a disguise.

Teams pour budget into ads to fix a leak they could patch for free — a homepage that doesn't tell visitors what to do.

Before you spend another dollar on acquisition, answer three questions:
1. Does your page say what you do in one line?
2. Is there one obvious next step?
3. Does it load in under 2 seconds on mobile?

If any is "no," fix that first. The math is simple: same traffic, more revenue.
