Most founders I talk to treat their website as a brochure. It should be your best salesperson.

Here's the shift that changes everything: a site that converts is not about looking good — it's about answering one question in the first 3 seconds: "What problem do you solve for me, and what do I do next?"

We built Softgrama around that principle. AI in the background, clarity in the foreground.

What's the one thing your site asks visitors to do? If you can't answer in a sentence, neither can they.
